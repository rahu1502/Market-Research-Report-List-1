<p><strong>The X-ray Foreign Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital Radiography Type</li><li>Computed Tomography Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-ray foreign detector market includes two main types: digital radiography and computed tomography. Digital radiography utilizes digital sensors to capture X-ray images, providing high-resolution and real-time imaging. This type is popular due to its cost-effectiveness, portability, and ease of use. On the other hand, computed tomography uses a rotating X-ray source and detectors to create cross-sectional images of an object, allowing for more detailed analysis and detection of foreign objects. This type is commonly used in medical imaging for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
