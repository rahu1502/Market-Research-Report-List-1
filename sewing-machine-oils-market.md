<p><p>Sewing machine oils are categorized into three market types: natural oil, mineral oil, and synthetic oil. Natural oil, as the name suggests, is derived from natural sources such as plants and animals. Mineral oil is a byproduct of petroleum distillation and is often used in sewing machines due to its high lubricity. Synthetic oil, on the other hand, is created through chemical processes to mimic the properties of natural and mineral oils. These different types of oils cater to diverse consumer preferences and sewing machine requirements.</p></p>
