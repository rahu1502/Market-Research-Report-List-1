<p><p>The wooden ceiling market is divided into various types, including linear wood, grill wood, tiles, panels, and custom shape wood. Linear wood ceilings feature long and straight wooden boards, creating a sleek and modern look. Grill wood ceilings consist of wooden slats arranged in a grid-like pattern, providing a more textured appearance. Tiles and panels wood ceilings use small wooden tiles or large wooden panels to cover the ceiling surface. Custom shape wood ceilings offer unique designs and can be tailored to suit specific architectural requirements, bringing creativity and individuality to the space.</p></p>
