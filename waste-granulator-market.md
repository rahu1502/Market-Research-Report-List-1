<p><p>Beside-the-press granulators are a type of waste granulator that are designed to be placed next to a processing machine for immediate recycling of waste plastic. They are compact and efficient, allowing for easy on-site recycling and reducing the amount of waste produced. </p><p>On the other hand, central granulators are larger machines that are used in recycling facilities to process large volumes of plastic waste. They are capable of handling a variety of materials and sizes, and are often used in industries that produce a significant amount of waste plastic, such as packaging or automotive manufacturing. These granulators are highly efficient and can process large amounts of waste material quickly.</p></p>
