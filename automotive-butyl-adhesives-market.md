<p><p>The automotive butyl adhesives market can be categorized into two main types: adhesive tape and adhesive paste. Adhesive tape refers to a thin strip of butyl adhesive material that is coated on one side with a release liner, which allows for easy application and removal. On the other hand, adhesive paste refers to a thicker butyl adhesive material that is applied using a dispensing gun or by hand. Both types of adhesive provide strong bonding properties and are commonly used in automotive applications such as panel bonding, seam sealing, and sound deadening.</p></p>
