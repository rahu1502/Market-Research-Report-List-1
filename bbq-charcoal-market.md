<p><ul><li>Charcoal Briquette</li><li>Hardwood Charcoal</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The BBQ charcoal market can be categorized into three main types: charcoal briquette, hardwood charcoal, and others. Charcoal briquettes are manufactured using compressed sawdust or other materials, often incorporating additives and binders. They are widely available, affordable, and provide a consistent heat source. Hardwood charcoal, on the other hand, is produced by burning hardwood in a low-oxygen environment. This type of charcoal offers a natural and smoky flavor to grilled food but can be more expensive. The "others" category includes niche products such as lump charcoal, flavored charcoal, and sustainable charcoal options.</p></p>
