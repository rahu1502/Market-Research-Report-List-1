<p><p>Potash fertilizer is available in two main market types: liquid and solid. Liquid potash fertilizer is typically a concentrated solution that is mixed with water before application. It offers ease of application and rapid absorption by plants. Solid potash fertilizer, on the other hand, is in the form of granules or powder. It is typically applied to the soil and slowly releases nutrients over time. Solid potash fertilizer is more cost-effective and has a longer-lasting effect compared to liquid fertilizer.</p></p>
