<p><strong>The Lubricanting Oil Additive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Viscosity Index Improvers</li><li>Dispersants</li><li>Detergents</li><li>Inhibitor/Antiwear Agents</li><li>Antioxidants</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lubricating oil additive market is segmented into different types based on their specific functions. Viscosity index improvers are additives that improve the oil's viscosity-temperature relationship. Dispersants prevent the formation of deposits and keep contaminants suspended. Detergents remove and control deposits from engine surfaces. Inhibitor/antiwear agents protect against wear and corrosion. Antioxidants prevent oil degradation due to oxidation. Lastly, there are other additives that serve various purposes such as pour point depressants and friction modifiers. These additives are essential for improving the performance and extending the lifespan of lubricating oils.</p></p>
