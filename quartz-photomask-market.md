The market is driven by the increasing demand for consumer electronics, such as smartphones, tablets, and electronic gadgets. The constant advancements in technology and the growing trend of miniaturization of electronic devices are contributing to the demand for smaller and complex integrated circuits, which require highly precise photomasks.</p><p>Additionally, the rising adoption of photomasks in the automotive industry for advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) and autonomous vehicles is further fueling the market's growth. Furthermore, the expanding application of photomasks in the healthcare industry, particularly in medical imaging devices and diagnostic equipment, is also boosting the market.</p><p>Moreover, the emergence of advanced technologies like artificial intelligence (AI), Internet of Things (IoT), and 5G will further augment the demand for photomasks in the semiconductor industry. These types are differentiated based on the origin of the quartz material used in their production.</p></p>
