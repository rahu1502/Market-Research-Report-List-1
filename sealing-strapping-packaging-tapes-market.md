<p><p>Sealing strapping packaging tapes are used to securely seal packages for shipping and transportation purposes. There are three main types of sealing strapping packaging tapes: rubber-based, acrylic, and hot melt adhesive. </p><p>Rubber-based tapes are known for their strong adhesion and flexibility, making them suitable for various surfaces and temperatures. Acrylic tapes offer excellent clarity and resistance to UV light, ensuring long-term durability. Finally, hot melt adhesive tapes use a synthetic rubber adhesive that offers superior bonding strength and quick adhesion even in colder temperatures. These different types of tapes cater to different packaging needs and provide varied benefits to users.</p></p>
