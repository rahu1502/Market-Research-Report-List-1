<p><p>Electronic Bone Growth Stimulators (BGS) are medical devices used to enhance the healing process of fractures and bone injuries. They work by emitting low-level electrical currents to stimulate bone cells, promoting bone growth and repair. Bone Growth Stimulation Devices, one type of BGS, are externally applied devices that deliver the electrical stimulation. Another type, known as Bone Morphogenetic Proteins (BMP), are naturally occurring substances that induce bone formation. Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) is another method used to promote bone healing, where concentrated platelets from a patient's blood are injected into the injured area.</p></p>
