<p><p>The nonwoven polyester fabric market consists of two types: single-layer and multilayer. Single-layer nonwoven polyester fabrics are made from a single sheet of polyester fibers, providing basic strength and stability. These fabrics are commonly used for disposable items like medical gowns and wipes. On the other hand, multilayer nonwoven polyester fabrics are made by bonding multiple layers of polyester fibers together. This creates a stronger fabric with enhanced filtration and insulation properties, making it suitable for applications such as automotive interiors and geotextiles.</p></p>
