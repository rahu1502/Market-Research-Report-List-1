<p><p>The liquid-filled transformer market can be classified into various types based on the type of liquid used. Seed oil-filled transformers utilize oils derived from plant seeds such as rapeseed or sunflower, offering excellent fire safety and environmental properties. Mineral oil-filled transformers, on the other hand, use mineral-based oils like naphthenic or paraffinic, providing improved insulation and cooling properties. Other liquid-filled transformers may employ different fluids, such as synthetic esters or silicone-based oils, incorporating specific advantages like biodegradability or enhanced thermal stability. These different types cater to diverse requirements and preferences of customers in the transformer industry.</p></p>
