<p><p>Resistance type optically clear adhesive refers to a type of adhesive that provides a strong bond between two surfaces by resisting the external forces acting on it, such as temperature changes, moisture, and mechanical stress. This adhesive is commonly used in touchscreen displays, LCD and OLED panels, and other electronic devices. Capacitance type optically clear adhesive, on the other hand, is used to bond two surfaces together while maintaining the capacitive touch function. It is often used in applications where touch sensitivity is required, such as smartphones, tablets, and touchscreens.</p></p>
