It has excellent chemical resistance and is commonly used in the manufacturing of clothing, home furnishings, and industrial fabrics. Diacetate Fiber, on the other hand, is created by treating cellulose with acetic acid. It possesses similar properties to Triacetate Fiber but is less chemically resistant. Diacetate Fiber is often utilized in the creation of garments and fashion accessories. Both types of acetate yarn have various applications in the textile industry.</p></p>
