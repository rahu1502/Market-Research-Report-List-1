<p><p>Reinforced plastics are a type of composite material that consists of a polymer matrix reinforced with fibers. The market for reinforced plastics can be categorized into several types. Carbon Fiber Reinforced Plastics (CFRP) utilize carbon fibers as the reinforcing material, providing high strength and stiffness. Glass Fiber Reinforced Plastics (GFRP) employ glass fibers, offering good strength and cost-effectiveness. Aramid Fiber Reinforced Plastics (AFRP) use aramid fibers for their outstanding resistance to impact and heat. The "Others" category encompasses other types of reinforcing fibers such as natural fibers or basalt fibers, which have specific properties suited for certain applications.</p></p>
