<p><p>A PM 2.5 protective face mask is a type of personal protective equipment (PPE) designed to filter out fine particles and pollutants in the air, particularly those with a diameter of 2.5 micrometers or smaller. These masks are used to protect wearers from inhaling harmful substances and are commonly used in industrial settings, construction sites, and areas with high air pollution levels.</p><p>The PM 2.5 protective face mask market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years.
