This type is commonly used for packaging applications where only one side requires printing or smoothness. On the other hand, Double Sided Super Calendered Kraft Paper is finished on both sides, providing a smooth surface on both sides. This type is preferred for applications where both sides may require printing or smoothness.</p></p>
