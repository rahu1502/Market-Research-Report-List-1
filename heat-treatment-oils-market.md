<p><strong>The Heat Treatment Oils Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Oils</li><li>Semi-hot Oils</li><li>Hot Oils</li><li>Bright Oils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heat treatment oils are used in various industrial processes to enhance the properties of steel products. Cold oils are primarily used for slow heating and cooling applications. Semi-hot oils are formulated for medium heating and quenching processes, offering improved hardness and strength. Hot oils, on the other hand, are designed for high-temperature processes, providing excellent thermal stability and oxidation resistance. Lastly, bright oils are used for the final stage of heat treatment, providing a clean, polished surface finish. These different types of oils cater to specific temperature requirements, ensuring optimal performance and quality of heat-treated materials.</p></p>

On the other hand, in massive quenching, the oils are employed to rapidly cool down large-sized components, thereby preventing the formation of unwanted structural transformations and ensuring improved mechanical properties.
