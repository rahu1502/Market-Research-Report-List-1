<p><p>Bio-nematicides are biological alternatives to chemical nematicides used for controlling nematodes in agriculture. Liquid bio-nematicides refer to formulations that are in a liquid state, typically containing live beneficial microorganisms that can suppress nematode populations. On the other hand, dry bio-nematicides are powdered or granulated formulations, often containing decomposed organic matter or beneficial fungi that can help in nematode control. Both liquid and dry bio-nematicides offer environmentally-friendly solutions for managing nematode pests, minimizing the use of synthetic chemicals and reducing the risk of negative impacts on ecosystems.</p></p>
