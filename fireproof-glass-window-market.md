<p><p>The fireproof glass window market encompasses three main types: fixed, movable, and hybrid. Fixed type windows are immovable and are commonly used in areas where permanent fire protection is needed. Movable type windows allow flexibility and can be easily opened or closed, making them suitable for spaces that require regular access or ventilation. Hybrid type windows combine elements of both fixed and movable types, providing a balance between fire resistance and functionality. Each type serves different needs while offering fire protection and safety in various settings.</p></p>
