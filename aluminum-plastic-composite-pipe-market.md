<p><strong>The Aluminum-plastic Composite Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PE/AL/PE</li><li>PE/AL/XPE</li><li>XPE/AL/XPE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum-plastic composite pipe market consists of different types such as PE/AL/PE, PE/AL/XPE, and XPE/AL/XPE. In the PE/AL/PE market, the pipe is made of an inner and outer layer of polyethylene (PE) with an aluminum layer in between. Similarly, in the PE/AL/XPE market, the pipe has an extra layer of cross-linked polyethylene (XPE) on top of the PE and aluminum layers. Lastly, in the XPE/AL/XPE market, the pipe has XPE layers on both sides of the aluminum layer. These variations offer different levels of strength, insulation, and durability for different applications.</p></p>
