<p><p>Electrical steel strips refer to thin sheets of magnetic steel utilized in various electrical devices. There are two main types: oriented electrical steel strips and non-oriented electrical steel strips. Oriented electrical steel strips are produced by controlling the crystal orientation, resulting in improved magnetic properties along a specific direction, making them suitable for power transformers. On the other hand, non-oriented electrical steel strips have random grain orientation, providing equal magnetic properties in all directions, thus making them ideal for applications such as electric motors and generators.</p></p>
