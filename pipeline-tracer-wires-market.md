<p><p>The pipeline tracer wires market consists of two main types: Pure Copper Conductor and Copper Clad Steel Conductor. Pure Copper Conductors are made entirely of copper, known for its high electrical conductivity and corrosion resistance. Copper Clad Steel Conductors, on the other hand, have a steel core covered with a layer of copper. This combination offers the strength of steel and the conductivity of copper. Both types of conductors are commonly used in pipeline tracing applications for their durability and ability to transmit electrical signals effectively.</p></p>
