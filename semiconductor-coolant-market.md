<p><p>The semiconductor coolant market is composed of two types: Perfluoroalkyl substances (PFAS) and Perfluoropolyethers (PFPE). PFAS is a type of coolant that consists of carbon and fluorine atoms, offering excellent thermal and chemical stability. PFPE, on the other hand, is a class of inert synthetic fluids with high thermal stability and low evaporation rate. Both types of coolants are widely used in the semiconductor industry to manage heat and ensure proper functioning of electronic devices.</p></p>
