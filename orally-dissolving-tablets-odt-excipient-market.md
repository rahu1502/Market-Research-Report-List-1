<p><p>Orally Dissolving Tablets (ODT) Excipient Market involves different types of excipients that are used in the formulation of orally dissolving tablets. Binders are substances that help in holding the tablet together and giving it the required shape and strength. Glidants are added to improve the flowability of the powdered ingredients. Diluents are used to increase the bulk of the tablet and help in achieving the desired weight. Disintegrants are added to promote the tablet's disintegration in the mouth. Sweeteners are used to enhance the taste of the tablet. Other excipients can include flavoring agents, colors, and lubricants, among others.</p></p>
