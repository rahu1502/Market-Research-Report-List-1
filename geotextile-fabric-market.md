<p><p>Geotextile fabric is widely used in various industries for its key properties such as filtration, separation, reinforcement, and erosion control. It is available in two main types: synthetic and natural. Synthetic geotextile fabrics are made from synthetic materials like polyester or polypropylene, providing high strength and durability. Natural geotextile fabrics, on the other hand, are typically made from plant-based materials like jute or coir, which offer biodegradability and environmentally friendly properties. Both types cater to the diverse needs of construction, transportation, agriculture, and environmental sectors, providing effective solutions for soil stabilization and water management.</p></p>
