<p><p>Flow cytometry instruments market can be categorized into two types: cell analyzers and cell sorters. Cell analyzers are used to measure and analyze various characteristics of cells, such as size, shape, and cell surface markers. These instruments are ideal for studying cell populations in research and diagnostics. On the other hand, cell sorters not only analyze cells but also separate them based on their properties. They utilize laser beams and sophisticated technology to identify and isolate specific cells, enabling advanced research and therapeutic applications.</p></p>
