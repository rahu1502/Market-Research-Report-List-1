<p><p>Distillation trays are key components used in distillation columns to separate liquid mixtures. Bubble cap trays contain small bubble caps that allow vapor to pass through, which enhances separation. Sieve trays consist of holes or slots, allowing liquid to pass through while vapor is trapped, promoting efficient separation. Valve trays include valves that can open or close to control the flow of vapor and liquid, optimizing separation. These different tray types cater to varying distillation requirements, providing options for industries seeking effective and tailored separation processes.</p></p>
