<p><p>The electric and hybrid vehicles driveline market encompasses various types of driveline technologies. E-CVT (Electric Continuously Variable Transmission) is a type of transmission that offers stepless gear ratio changes, providing smooth acceleration and improved fuel efficiency. Automatic Transmission (AT) is a traditional transmission system that automatically shifts gears without driver intervention. Dual Clutch Transmission (DCT) combines the efficiency of manual transmission with the convenience of automatic transmission by using two separate clutches for odd and even gear sets, reducing power loss during gear shifts.</p></p>
