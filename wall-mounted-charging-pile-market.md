<p><p>The Wall-mounted Charging Pile market consists of two main types: AC Charging Pile and DC Charging Pile. AC Charging Pile is designed to convert alternating current into direct current, making it suitable for charging electric vehicles (EVs) at a slower rate. On the other hand, DC Charging Pile directly provides high-voltage direct current to rapidly charge EVs. AC Charging Piles are commonly found in residential areas, while DC Charging Piles are typically installed in public spaces like parking lots or along highways, providing faster charging options for EV users.</p></p>
