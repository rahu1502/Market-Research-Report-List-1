<p><p>The electric water pump (EWP) market consists of two main types: 12V EWP and 24V EWP. The 12V EWP refers to a water pump that operates on a 12-volt electrical system, commonly found in most vehicles. It is designed to efficiently circulate coolant to prevent engine overheating. On the other hand, the 24V EWP operates on a 24-volt electrical system, which is commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles or industrial applications. Both types of EWPs serve the purpose of cooling engines, but they differ in voltage requirements depending on the application they are intended for.</p></p>
