<p><p>Injection molded plastics is a manufacturing process that involves injecting molten material, typically plastic, into a mold cavity. The molten plastic is forced into the mold through a nozzle, ensuring the material takes the shape of the mold, and then it is cooled and solidified to produce the final product. This process is widely used in industries such as automotive, packaging, consumer goods, electronics, and healthcare.</p><p>The global injection molded plastics market is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for lightweight and durable products across various industries. The market analysis indicates that factors such as technological advancements in the manufacturing process, rising disposable income, and surging demand for packaging and automotive components are contributing to market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing adoption of injection molded plastics in the healthcare industry due to their ability to provide sterilization, safety, and cost-effectiveness is also propelling the market growth. Additionally, the growing trend of sustainable packaging and the increasing popularity of electric vehicles are expected to further boost the demand for injection molded plastics.</p><p>Moreover, the market is witnessing several trends that are shaping its landscape.
