<p><p>A steering wheel armature, also known as a steering column, is a component in a vehicle that connects the steering wheel to the steering mechanism. It allows the driver to control the direction of the vehicle by transmitting the rotation of the steering wheel to the wheels. The armature typically consists of a shaft, a set of gears, and various joints and linkages.</p><p>The steering wheel armature market is currently experiencing significant growth and is expected to continue expanding in the future. The increasing demand for vehicles, especially in developing countries, is a major driving factor for the market. Additionally, the rising emphasis on vehicle safety and comfort features is fueling the demand for advanced steering systems, which in turn is boosting the market growth for steering wheel armatures.</p><p>Furthermore, the integration of advanced technologies like power steering, tilt and telescopic adjustments, and electronic control units (ECUs) is propelling the growth of the market. These technologies enhance the overall functionality and ease of use of the steering system, resulting in increased customer satisfaction and demand.</p><p>Moreover, the market is witnessing a shift towards electric vehicles (EVs) and autonomous vehicles, which is further augmenting the demand for steering wheel armatures. EVs require specialized armatures for electric power steering systems, while autonomous vehicles rely on advanced steering mechanisms for precise maneuvering.</p><p>Overall, with the growing automotive industry and the increasing adoption of advanced steering technologies, the steering wheel armature market is expected to witness steady growth in the coming years. The market is projected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 10.2% during the forecasted period.</p></p>
