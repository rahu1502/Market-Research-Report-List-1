<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The multi-axis motion controller market consists of two types: stand-alone and bus-type controllers. Stand-alone multi-axis motion controllers are independent devices that function without the need for a separate computer or network. They are suitable for basic applications with limited complexity. On the other hand, bus-type multi-axis motion controllers are connected to a computer via a bus network, allowing for more complex and precise motion control. These controllers are commonly used in applications requiring advanced functionalities and extensive coordination between multiple axes.</p></p>
