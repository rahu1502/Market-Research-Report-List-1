<p><p>A Graphite Synthetic Furnace is a high-temperature furnace used in the production of synthetic graphite. The market is being driven by the increasing demand for synthetic graphite in various industries, including steel, aerospace, electronics, and energy storage. Synthetic graphite offers superior properties such as high thermal conductivity, electrical conductivity, and mechanical strength, making it ideal for use in these demanding applications.</p><p>Moreover, the growing popularity of electric vehicles (EVs) is also a significant factor propelling the growth of the graphite synthetic furnace market.
