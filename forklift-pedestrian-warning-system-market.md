<p><p>The Forklift Pedestrian Warning System market offers two main types: RFID Based and UWB Based systems. RFID Based systems use radio frequency identification technology to track and monitor the movement of pedestrians. This system includes RFID tags worn by pedestrians and RFID readers installed on forklifts to detect their presence and issue warnings. On the other hand, UWB Based systems utilize ultra-wideband technology for accurate and real-time tracking of pedestrians near forklifts. These systems employ UWB transmitters and receivers to provide precise location data and ensure pedestrian safety.</p></p>
