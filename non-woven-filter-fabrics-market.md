<p><strong>The Non-woven Filter Fabrics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Needle Punched Nonwoven</li><li>Spunbond Nonwoven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-woven filter fabrics market consists of two main types: needle punched nonwovens and spunbond nonwovens. Needle punched nonwovens are produced by mechanically interlocking fibers, creating a dense and strong structure. They are widely used in various industries for their high filtration efficiency and flexibility. On the other hand, spunbond nonwovens are manufactured by extruding continuous filaments of thermoplastic polymers, resulting in a uniform and durable fabric. These fabrics are commonly utilized in applications where strength and resistance to chemicals are important requirements.</p></p>
