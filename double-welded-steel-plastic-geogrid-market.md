<p><p>The double welded steel plastic geogrid market consists of two main types: biaxial tension and uniaxial tension. Biaxial tension geogrids have equal strength in both directions, offering reliable reinforcement for soils, preventing lateral spreading, and improving load distribution. On the other hand, uniaxial tension geogrids are primarily designed for reinforcement in one direction, ideal for applications where the primary load is expected to occur only in a certain direction. Both types provide enhanced strength and stability to soil structures, ensuring durability and effective performance in various engineering projects.</p></p>
