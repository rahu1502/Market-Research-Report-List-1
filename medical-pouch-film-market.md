<p><p>The medical pouch film market offers various types of films used in the packaging of medical products. Aluminum foil film provides excellent barrier properties against moisture and gases. BOPP film (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) offers high clarity and good tensile strength. BOPA film (Biaxially Oriented Polyamide) offers excellent puncture resistance and barrier properties. BOPET film (Biaxially Oriented Polyethylene Terephthalate) provides high tensile strength and excellent gloss. CPP film (Cast Polypropylene) offers good moisture resistance and flexibility. LLDPE film (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) provides excellent cling and tear resistance. Lastly, the "Other" market category includes various specialized films used for specific medical packaging purposes.</p></p>
