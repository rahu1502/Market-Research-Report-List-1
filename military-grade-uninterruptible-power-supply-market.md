<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Military Grade Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) market consists of three major types: Passive Standby, On-line, and Line-Interactive. Passive Standby UPS is a basic form that kicks in only when the main power source fails; it offers limited protection against power disruptions. On-line UPS operates continuously, ensuring a consistent power supply by running off battery power. It filters out any power issues like voltage fluctuations. Line-Interactive UPS integrates the features of both passive standby and on-line types. It provides intermediate protection against power disturbances and offers battery backup during outages.</p></p>
