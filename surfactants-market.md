<p><p>Surfactants are chemical compounds used in various industries for their ability to lower surface tension between liquids or facilitate the mixing of substances. Anionic surfactants have a negative charge and are commonly used in detergents and cleaning products. Amphoteric surfactants have both positive and negative charges and are gentle on the skin, making them suitable for personal care items. Cationic surfactants carry a positive charge and are frequently utilized in fabric softeners and hair conditioners. Nonionic surfactants have no charge and are versatile, found in a wide range of applications including pharmaceuticals and pesticides.</p></p>
