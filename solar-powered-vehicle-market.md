<p><p>The Solar Powered Vehicle market includes two main types: Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) and Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEVs). BEVs are fully powered by electricity stored in a rechargeable battery and do not rely on any internal combustion engine. These vehicles are designed to reduce environmental impact by using renewable energy sources and promoting fuel efficiency.</p></p>
