<p><p>The Nutritional Yeast Powder market can be categorized into four types: Active Dry Yeast, Spent Yeast, Yeast Derivates, and Others. Active Dry Yeast is a form of yeast that is deactivated and dried for storage purposes. Spent Yeast is the byproduct obtained after the fermentation process. Yeast Derivates are the extracted compounds from yeast, such as yeast extracts or autolysates. The "Others" category includes specialized yeast products for specific applications, like brewer's yeast or torula yeast. These different types offer various benefits and uses in the food and beverage industry.</p></p>
