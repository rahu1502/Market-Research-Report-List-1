<p><p>Glass-to-Metal Connectors Market Types include Matched Connectors/Seals and Compression Connectors/Seals. Matched Connectors/Seals involve a precise glass and metal match, where the coefficient of thermal expansion (CTE) is adjusted to ensure optimal performance under varying temperatures. This type provides exceptional hermetic sealing and is commonly used in aerospace and defense applications. On the other hand, Compression Connectors/Seals utilize a compression mechanism to provide a reliable seal between glass and metal components. These connectors offer flexibility and are widely used in industries such as telecommunications and electronics.</p></p>
