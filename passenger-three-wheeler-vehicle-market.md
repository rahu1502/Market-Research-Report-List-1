<p><p>The Passenger Three-Wheeler Vehicle market consists of two main types: Front Engine and Rear Engine. This type is commonly used for transportation services and cargo deliveries. On the other hand, the Rear Engine market has the engine located at the rear of the vehicle, offering better traction and load-carrying capacity. This type is popular in passenger transport and is often more fuel-efficient. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the passenger three-wheeler market.</p></p>
