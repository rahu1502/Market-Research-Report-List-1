<p><strong>The Arc Light Electric Welding Electrode Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Consumable Welding Electrode</li><li>Non-consumable Welding Electrode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Arc Light Electric Welding Electrode market is divided into two main types: Consumable Welding Electrode and Non-consumable Welding Electrode. Consumable Welding Electrodes are designed to be consumed during the welding process, meaning they are continuously melted and used up. They are used for materials that can conduct electricity, such as steel and iron. On the other hand, Non-consumable Welding Electrodes are not consumed during welding, as they are made of high melting point materials like tungsten or graphite. They do not melt and are mainly used for welding non-conductive materials like aluminum or stainless steel.</p></p>
