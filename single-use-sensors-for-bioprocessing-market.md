<p><p>Single-use sensors for bioprocessing are essential tools used in the biopharmaceutical industry to monitor and control various parameters during the production process. Conductivity sensors measure the electrical conductivity of a liquid, indicating its concentration or purity. Flow sensors monitor the flow rate of fluids within the system. pH sensors measure the acidity or alkalinity of a solution. Pressure sensors gauge the pressure in the system. Temperature sensors track the temperature, ensuring optimal conditions for bioprocessing. These sensors offer convenience and cost-efficiency as they are disposable after a single use, reducing contamination risks and eliminating the need for cleaning and maintenance.</p></p>
