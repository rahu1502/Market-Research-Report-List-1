<p><p>The intelligent chassis market consists of three types: L2, L3, and L4. In an L2 market, the chassis provides partial automation, offering functions like lane-keeping and adaptive cruise control, but still requires human supervision. L3 vehicles are more advanced, with the chassis capable of taking full control under certain conditions, but still reliant on human intervention. L4 market represents highly autonomous vehicles, where the chassis can perform all driving tasks without human intervention, though it may have limitations within specific environments or situations.</p></p>
