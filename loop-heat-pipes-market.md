<p><p>Loop Heat Pipes (LHPs) are two-phase heat transfer devices that are used to transfer heat with high efficiency over long distances. They consist of a sealed tube or loop that is partially filled with a working fluid. The loop is composed of an evaporator, condenser, and a capillary pump, which work together to transfer heat from the heat source to the heat sink. LHPs are used in various applications such as spacecraft thermal control systems, electronics cooling, energy recovery systems, and heat pipe-assisted heat exchangers.</p><p>The future outlook for the Loop Heat Pipes Market is expected to be positive, with a projected Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of 6.3% during the forecast period.
