<p><p>Acrylic emulsions are water-based paints or coatings that are made by combining acrylic polymers with water and other additives. The market is expected to witness significant growth, primarily driven by the increasing demand for eco-friendly paints and coatings. With growing concerns about the harmful effects of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) emitted by solvent-based paints, there is a shift towards water-based alternatives like acrylic emulsions. Moreover, the growing construction and automotive sectors in developing economies are also expected to contribute to market growth.</p><p>The current outlook for the acrylic emulsions market is positive as well.
