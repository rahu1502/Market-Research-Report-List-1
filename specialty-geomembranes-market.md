<p><p>The specialty geomembranes market comprises two main types: high-density polyethylene (HDPE) and low-density polyethylene (LDPE). HDPE geomembranes are recognized for their high strength and excellent chemical resistance, making them suitable for applications requiring long-term durability, such as waste containment and mining. LDPE geomembranes, on the other hand, are characterized by their flexibility and low thermal conductivity, which make them ideal for applications like agriculture and environmental protection. Both types of geomembranes play a crucial role in various industries by providing impermeable barriers to prevent leakage and contamination.</p></p>
