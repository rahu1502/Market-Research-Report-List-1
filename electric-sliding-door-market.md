<p><p>Electric sliding doors are a type of door that can be opened or closed automatically using electricity. There are two main market types for electric sliding doors: top-mounted type and ground rail type. In the top-mounted type, the door is suspended from a track that is fixed on the top of the door frame. This allows for a smooth opening and closing motion. On the other hand, in the ground rail type, the door runs on a track that is installed on the ground, providing stability and durability. Both types offer convenient and efficient operation for various applications.</p></p>
