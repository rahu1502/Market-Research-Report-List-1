<p><p>An Active Degaussing System is a technology used to reduce or eliminate the magnetic field of a ship, thus reducing its susceptibility to magnetic mines. There are two types of Active Degaussing Systems: External Degaussing Systems and Shipboard Degaussing Systems. </p><p>External Degaussing Systems are installed outside the ship's hull and generate an equal and opposite magnetic field to neutralize the ship's own magnetic field. This technology is typically used in surface ships or submarines. </p><p>Shipboard Degaussing Systems, on the other hand, are installed within the ship's hull and are used to generate magnetic fields to counterbalance the ship's own magnetic field. This technology is mainly implemented in naval vessels to protect them from magnetic mines.</p></p>
