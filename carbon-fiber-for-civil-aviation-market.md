<p><p>Carbon fiber is a lightweight and durable material used extensively in the civil aviation industry. There are three main types of carbon fiber used in this sector: PAN-based carbon fiber, viscose-based carbon fiber, and pitch-based carbon fiber. PAN-based carbon fiber is made from a polymer called polyacrylonitrile and is the most widely used type due to its high strength and excellent mechanical properties. Viscose-based carbon fiber, derived from cellulose, offers lower cost and decent performance. Lastly, pitch-based carbon fiber, made from petroleum or coal tar pitch, provides exceptional stiffness and strength but is less common in civil aviation applications.</p></p>
