It has three market types: isotactic polypropylene, atactic polypropylene, and syndiotactic polypropylene. Isotactic polypropylene has a highly ordered molecular structure, providing excellent mechanical properties, stability, and heat resistance. Atactic polypropylene has a random molecular structure, resulting in a soft and sticky material used in adhesives and sealants. Syndiotactic polypropylene has a partially ordered molecular structure and offers high stiffness and heat resistance, making it suitable for applications like automotive parts and packaging materials.</p></p>
