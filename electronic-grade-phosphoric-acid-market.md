<p><p>Electronic Grade Phosphoric Acid is divided into two market types: Panel Level and IC Level. Panel Level refers to the application of phosphoric acid in the production of flat panel displays, such as LCD and OLED screens. It is used as a glass etchant and a surface treatment for thin-film transistor (TFT) arrays. On the other hand, IC Level refers to the use of phosphoric acid in the manufacturing of integrated circuits, such as microchips. It is employed as a surface cleaning agent to remove contamination and impurities during chip fabrication processes.</p></p>
