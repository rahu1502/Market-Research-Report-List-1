Polyether-based TPU provides exceptional flexibility and low-temperature resistance, making it ideal for automotive interior components and footwear. Polycaprolactone-based TPU combines the advantages of both polyester and polyether, offering high resilience and durability, often used in applications such as conveyor belts and automotive seals.
