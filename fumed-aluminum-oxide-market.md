<p><p>Fumed aluminum oxide, also known as amorphous aluminum oxide, is a highly pure form of aluminum oxide that is produced through the process of flame hydrolysis. The increasing demand for high-performance materials in industries such as automotive, aerospace, electronics, and construction is expected to drive the market growth. Fumed aluminum oxide is widely used as a reinforcing filler in polymers and elastomers, improving their mechanical properties and enhancing their resistance to chemicals and heat.</p><p>Additionally, the growing demand for electronic devices and the rapid expansion of the electronics industry are expected to contribute to market growth. Fumed aluminum oxide is extensively used in electronic components such as capacitors, integrated circuits, and electronic substrates due to its excellent dielectric properties and thermal stability.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing a trend of technological advancements in the manufacturing process of fumed aluminum oxide.
