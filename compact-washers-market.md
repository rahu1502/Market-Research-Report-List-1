<p><p>Compact washers are available in two main market types: top load and front load. Top load washers have a vertically oriented drum and users can load or unload laundry from the top. This design makes it easy to add or remove items even during a wash cycle. On the other hand, front load washers have a horizontally oriented drum and laundry is loaded from the front. Front load washers typically offer larger capacities and are known for being more energy-efficient and gentle on clothes.</p></p>
