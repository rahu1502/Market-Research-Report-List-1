<p><p>Inkjet paper market types refer to different finishes and qualities of paper that are specifically designed for inkjet printers. Matte paper offers a non-reflective surface, making it ideal for text-based documents and photographs without a glossy finish. Glossy paper has a reflective coating that enhances color saturation and provides a shiny appearance, making it suitable for high-quality photo prints. Semi-gloss paper falls between matte and glossy, offering a moderate shine while reducing glare. Other market types may include specialty papers like canvas or fine art paper that cater to specific printing needs such as art reproductions or signage.</p></p>
