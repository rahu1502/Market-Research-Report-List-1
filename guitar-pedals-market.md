<p><p>Guitar pedals are electronic devices used by guitarists to enhance or modify their sound. The market for guitar pedals can be categorized into various types. Gain effects are used to increase the volume and distortion of the guitar signal. Compressors help in balancing the dynamics of the signal. Pitch effects manipulate the pitch of the sound, whereas volume pedals control the volume levels. Filter effects modify the tonal characteristics of the guitar. Time-based effects include delay and reverb, adding depth and ambiance to the sound. Other pedals cater to unique effects like modulation, wah-wah, octave, and more.</p></p>
