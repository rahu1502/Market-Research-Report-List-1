<p><p>A curing agent for epoxy resin is a substance that is used to speed up the curing or hardening process of epoxy resin. Epoxy resin is a versatile and widely used material in various industries such as construction, automotive, aerospace, electronics, and others due to its excellent properties of adhesion, chemical resistance, and mechanical strength. However, in order to achieve its full potential, epoxy resin needs to be cured with a suitable curing agent.</p><p>The curing agent reacts with the epoxy resin and initiates a chemical reaction that transforms the resin from a liquid to a solid state. This process is crucial as it determines the final mechanical and thermal properties of the cured resin. Curing agents can be divided into two types - reactive curing agents and non-reactive curing agents. Reactive curing agents undergo a chemical reaction with the epoxy resin, while non-reactive curing agents act as catalysts to promote the curing process.</p><p>The future outlook of the curing agent for epoxy resin market looks promising.
