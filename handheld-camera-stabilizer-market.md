<p><strong>The Handheld Camera Stabilizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Collapsible</li><li>Not Foldable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld camera stabilizer market includes two main types: collapsible and not foldable stabilizers. Collapsible stabilizers are designed to be easily collapsed or folded down for compact storage and portability when not in use. On the other hand, not foldable stabilizers lack the capability to be folded or collapsed, offering a more rigid and sturdy structure. Both types serve the purpose of reducing camera shake and providing smooth footage, but collapsible stabilizers prioritize convenience and portability, while not foldable stabilizers prioritize stability and durability.</p></p>
