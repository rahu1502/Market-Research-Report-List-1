<p><p>PVDC Active Food Packaging refers to food packaging materials made from Polyvinylidene Chloride, a type of plastic. There are two main types of PVDC used in this market: PVDC Latex and PVDC Resin. PVDC Latex is a polymer dispersion that offers excellent barrier properties against moisture, oxygen, and other contaminants. It is often used in coating applications for food packaging films. PVDC Resin, on the other hand, is a solid form of PVDC that is typically used to produce rigid packaging materials such as bottles or containers. Both types of PVDC contribute to the shelf life extension and preservation of food products.</p></p>
