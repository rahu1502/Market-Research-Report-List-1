<p><p>The straight-cut gears market is categorized into three types: cylindrical, umbrella, and others. Cylindrical gears are the most common type, characterized by their straight teeth that are parallel to the gear axis. Umbrella gears consist of two sets of straight-cut gears placed at 90-degree angles to each other, allowing them to transmit power at right angles. The "others" category includes unique gear designs that do not fit into the cylindrical or umbrella classifications. These gears are used in various industries, including automotive, machinery, and transportation, to transfer rotational motion efficiently and precisely.</p></p>
