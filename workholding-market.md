Grinding workholding focuses on magnetic chucks, collets, and fixtures designed for grinding machines to securely hold and position the workpiece during abrasive operations. EDM workholding includes fixtures, vises, and clamps designed for electrical discharge machines, providing stability and precision during the machining process.</p></p>
