<p><p>The sewage truck market is divided into three types based on their loading mechanisms: front loaders, rear loaders, and side loaders. Front loaders come equipped with a hydraulic arm in the front that lifts and empties the waste into the truck. Rear loaders have a rear-accessed container that can be lifted and dumped hydraulically. Side loaders, as the name suggests, feature a mechanism on the side of the truck to collect waste. These different types cater to varying operational preferences and space restrictions in different areas.</p></p>
