<p><p>Spinnakers are types of sails used in sailing. Symmetric spinnakers are designed to be used when sailing downwind with the wind behind the boat. They have equal dimensions on both sides and require the use of a spinnaker pole to hold them out. Asymmetric spinnakers, on the other hand, are more versatile and can be used when sailing at different angles. They have different dimensions on each side and do not require a pole to be used. Both types of spinnakers are commonly used in racing and cruising sailboats.</p></p>
