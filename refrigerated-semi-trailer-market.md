<p><p>The refrigerated semi-trailer market consists of two main types: single temperature refrigerated semi-trailers and multi-temperature refrigerated semi-trailers. Single temperature refrigerated semi-trailers are designed to maintain a uniform temperature throughout the trailer, typically used for transporting goods that require a constant temperature. On the other hand, multi-temperature refrigerated semi-trailers are equipped with different compartments, each with its own temperature control, enabling the transportation of varied goods that require different temperature settings. These trailers offer flexibility and efficiency in transporting a wide range of perishable goods.</p></p>
