<p><p>An in-flight entertainment (IFE) device refers to any electronic device that offers entertainment content to passengers during a flight. <p><p>The In-flight Entertainment (IFE) device market can be categorized into two types: stationary and handheld devices. They offer a wide range of entertainment options, such as movies, TV shows, games, and music, providing a seamless experience throughout the flight. On the other hand, handheld IFE devices are portable gadgets that passengers can carry onboard. These devices, like tablets or smartphones, allow users to access various entertainment content and may require their own internet connection.</p></p>
