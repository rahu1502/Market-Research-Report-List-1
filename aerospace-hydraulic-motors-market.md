<p><p>Aerospace hydraulic motors are classified into two main types: aerospace bent hydraulic motors and aerospace inline hydraulic motors. Aerospace bent hydraulic motors have a bent axis design where the motor shaft is at an angle to the axis of the cylinder block. This design allows for compact and lightweight construction while providing high torque and power density. Aerospace inline hydraulic motors have a straight axis design, making them suitable for applications where space is limited. Both types are essential components in aerospace systems, offering efficient and reliable hydraulic power transmission.</p></p>
