The growing need for fuel-efficient aircraft is expected to drive the demand for h-BNCC in this sector.</p><p>The electronics industry is also witnessing a surge in demand for h-BNCC due to its high thermal conductivity and dielectric properties. In the Graphite Structure type, the boron nitride particles are arranged in a hexagonal lattice similar to graphite, providing excellent lubricity, thermal conductivity, and electrical insulation. Both types serve diverse applications in industries such as electronics, automotive, aerospace, and energy.</p></p>
