<p><p>The aquatic herbicides market comprises different types of herbicides, namely Glyphosate, Imazapyr, Diquat, and Triclopyr. Glyphosate is a widely used herbicide that effectively controls submerged, floating, and emergent weeds. Imazapyr is known for its long-lasting effects in controlling both terrestrial and aquatic plants. Diquat is a non-selective herbicide that rapidly kills a wide range of aquatic plants. Lastly, Triclopyr is commonly used for managing unwanted vegetation in aquatic environments. Each herbicide has its specific characteristics and target plants, making them suitable for different aquatic weed control purposes.</p></p>
