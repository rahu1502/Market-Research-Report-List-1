<p><p>Chitosan is a natural compound derived from the shells of crustaceans, such as shrimp and crab. In the cosmetics industry, chitosan is used in skincare products for its moisturizing and antimicrobial properties. It is also utilized in the food and beverages industry as a natural preservative and thickening agent. Additionally, chitosan has shown potential in agriculture as a biopesticide and plant growth enhancer.</p><p>The future outlook of the chitosan market appears to be promising.
