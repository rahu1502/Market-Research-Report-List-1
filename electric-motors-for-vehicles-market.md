<p><strong>The Electric Motors for Vehicles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motor</li><li>Asynchronous Motor</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Electric motors for vehicles can be categorized into two types: Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motor (PMSM) and Asynchronous Motor. PMSMs use a permanent magnet in the rotor, resulting in higher power density and improved efficiency. They are commonly used in hybrid and electric vehicles due to their compact size and higher torque capabilities. On the other hand, Asynchronous Motors do not rely on magnets and are less expensive to produce. Although they have a lower power density than PMSMs, they are commonly used in electric vehicles that require less torque, such as small city cars.</p></p>
