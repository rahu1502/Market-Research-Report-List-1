<p><p>Polyurethane Based Coating Hardener market is classified into two types: Water-Based and Solvent-Based. Water-Based coating hardeners are formulated using water as a dispersing agent, making them environmentally friendly and easy to clean. They have low VOC emissions and are suitable for indoor applications. Solvent-Based coating hardeners, on the other hand, use organic solvents as dispersants. They offer faster drying times, enhanced durability, and are commonly used for outdoor applications. The choice between Water-Based and Solvent-Based hardeners depends on factors like application requirements, environmental concerns, and desired performance characteristics.</p></p>
