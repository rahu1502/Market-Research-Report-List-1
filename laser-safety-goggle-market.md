<p><p>The Laser Safety Goggle market consists of two main types: Absorbent Goggles and Reflective Goggles. Absorbent Goggles are designed to absorb the laser radiation and convert it into heat, preventing it from reaching the eyes. These goggles are suitable for low-powered lasers. Reflective Goggles, on the other hand, have a reflective coating that deflects the laser radiation away from the eyes. They are used for higher-powered lasers. Both types play a crucial role in protecting the eyes from harmful laser radiation in various industries such as healthcare, research, and manufacturing.</p></p>
