<p><p>Bicarbonate market types include crystal, powder, wet solid, and liquid forms. Crystal bicarbonate is composed of larger solid particles, while powder bicarbonate is finely ground for easy dissolution. Wet solid bicarbonate contains some moisture, making it more prone to clumping. Finally, liquid bicarbonate is a solution where bicarbonate is dissolved in water. Each type has its unique characteristics and applications, such as crystal and powder forms being used in baking and cooking, while liquid form is commonly used in medical treatments and industrial processes.</p></p>
