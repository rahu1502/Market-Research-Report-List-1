<p><p>Dimethylsulphoxide (DMSO) is an organic compound with the formula (CH3)2SO. It is a colorless liquid that has a variety of industrial and pharmaceutical applications due to its unique properties. DMSO is a highly polar and aprotic solvent, meaning it can dissolve a wide range of substances and does not donate or accept protons during chemical reactions. This makes it an excellent solvent for a variety of applications such as the manufacturing of drugs, polymers, and agrochemicals.</p><p>The market for DMSO is expected to witness substantial growth during the forecast period. The increasing demand for DMSO in the pharmaceutical industry is one of the key factors driving market growth. DMSO is widely used as a solvent and a cryoprotectant in the production of drugs, especially in the development of new therapeutic compounds. Its ability to enhance the penetration of drugs through the skin has also contributed to its increased usage in transdermal drug delivery systems.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing application of DMSO in the manufacturing of paints, coatings, and adhesives is fueling market growth. DMSO acts as a highly efficient solvent in these industries, providing enhanced performance and improved properties to the final products.
