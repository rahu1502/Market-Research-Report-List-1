<p><p>Gravity conveyors are a type of material handling equipment used to move items or products from one place to another without the need for motorized power. They rely on the force of gravity to move items down an inclined or declined surface. The main components of gravity conveyors include rollers or skatewheels that support and propel the products, frames or supports that provide stability, and various accessories such as connectors, gates, and stops.</p><p>The current outlook of the gravity conveyors market is positive and shows promising growth prospects. Increasing demand for efficient and cost-effective material handling solutions in industries such as manufacturing, logistics, and e-commerce is driving the market growth. Gravity conveyors offer several benefits such as easy installation, low maintenance requirements, versatility, and the ability to handle a wide range of products.</p><p>In addition, technological advancements in gravity conveyors, such as the integration of sensors and automation capabilities, are further fueling market growth. These innovations improve the overall productivity, efficiency, and safety of material handling operations.
