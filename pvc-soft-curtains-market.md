<p><p>The PVC soft curtains market encompasses three main types: Clear PVC Curtains, Ultra-Clear PVC Curtains, and Opacity PVC Curtains. Ultra-Clear PVC Curtains have enhanced transparency and offer excellent clarity, making them ideal for spaces with higher aesthetic requirements. Opacity PVC Curtains, however, are designed to restrict light and maintain privacy, often used in industrial settings or areas where limited visibility is desired.</p></p>
