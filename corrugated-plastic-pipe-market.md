<p><p>The corrugated plastic pipe market consists of two main types: single-wall corrugated pipe and double-wall corrugated pipe. Single-wall corrugated pipe is a flexible pipe with a single layer of corrugations, allowing for easy installation and cost-effectiveness. It is commonly used for drainage applications, such as agricultural, residential, or commercial purposes. On the other hand, double-wall corrugated pipe is a stronger pipe with two layers of corrugations. It offers enhanced strength and durability, making it suitable for demanding applications like stormwater management or underground sewers.</p></p>
