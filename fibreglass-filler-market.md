<p><p>Fibreglass filler is a type of filler used in the automotive and construction industries to repair or fill gaps and imperfections in fibreglass surfaces. There are two main types: 1K and 2K. 1K fibreglass filler is a single-component product, requiring no additional catalyst or hardener for it to cure. On the other hand, 2K fibreglass filler is a two-component product that needs a catalyst or hardener to initiate the curing process. Both types provide strong adhesion, flexibility, and durability, making them ideal for fibreglass repairs.</p></p>
