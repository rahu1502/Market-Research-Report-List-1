<p><p>The sustainable barrier paper market includes two main types: single-sided coating and double-sided coating. Single-sided coating refers to paper that has been coated with a barrier material on one side only, whereas double-sided coating means that both sides of the paper have been coated. These coatings are designed to provide a barrier against moisture, oil, grease, and other substances. Single-sided coating is commonly used in applications where only one side of the paper requires protection, while double-sided coating is suitable for applications where both sides need to be protected.</p></p>
