<p><p>Fishmeal feed is categorized into two types: Steam Dried (SD) and Flame Dried (FD). Steam Dried fishmeal is obtained by drying the raw fish under high-pressure steam, ensuring the temperature remains low to maintain the nutritional value. On the other hand, Flame Dried fishmeal is produced by allowing the raw fish to come in direct contact with hot gases, which helps in preserving the essential nutrients. Both SD and FD fishmeal feed offer a nutrient-rich source for animal consumption, with the main difference being the drying methods used during production.</p></p>
