<p><ul><li>Dihydric Alcohol</li><li>Trihydric Alcohol</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soy polyol market is divided into three types: dihydric alcohol, trihydric alcohol, and others. Dihydric alcohol refers to a type of soy polyol that contains two hydroxyl groups and is commonly used in the production of polyester resins and polyurethane foams. Trihydric alcohol, on the other hand, contains three hydroxyl groups, making it suitable for applications in the manufacture of polyesters, polyurethanes, and plasticizers. The category labeled as "others" includes any soy polyol that does not fall into the dihydric or trihydric alcohol category and is typically utilized in various industrial sectors.</p></p>
