<p><p>Polycarbonate panels are widely used in construction and industrial applications due to their durability, transparency, and lightweight nature. There are three main types of polycarbonate panels in the market: multi-wall panels, corrugated panels, and solid panels. Multi-wall panels consist of multiple layers which provide excellent insulation properties. Corrugated panels are designed with a wave-like structure, making them suitable for roofing and siding applications. Solid panels are dense and offer high impact resistance, making them ideal for glazing, security, and noise reduction purposes.</p></p>
