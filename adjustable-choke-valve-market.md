<p><p>The adjustable choke valve market consists of various types of valves used in different industries and applications. The needle type adjustable choke valve (JFK) has a conical needle that controls the flow by adjusting its position. The barrel type choke valve (JFT) has a cylindrical shape with openings that can be adjusted to regulate the flow. The cage type choke valve (JFL) uses a cage-like structure to control the flow. The orifice type choke valve (JFB) has a small orifice that restricts the flow. Other types of adjustable choke valves also exist in the market.</p></p>
