<p><p>The auto dimming rear view mirror market consists of two types: outer auto dimming mirrors and inside auto dimming mirrors. Outer auto dimming mirrors are installed on the exterior of the vehicle and automatically adjust their brightness according to the surrounding light conditions. Inside auto dimming mirrors are located inside the vehicle and function similarly by dimming the reflection from the rear windshield to reduce glare from headlights of vehicles behind. Both these types aim to enhance driver visibility and safety by reducing distractions caused by bright lights.</p></p>
