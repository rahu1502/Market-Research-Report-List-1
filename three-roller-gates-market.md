<p><p>Three roller gates market types include mechanical, semi-automatic, and automatic options. Mechanical roller gates are operated manually, requiring physical effort to open and close. Semi-automatic roller gates incorporate both manual and automated features, where users need to assist with the gate's movement, but some processes are automated, such as gate locking and unlocking. Automatic roller gates are completely automated, driven by motor systems and sensors, and can be controlled remotely.
