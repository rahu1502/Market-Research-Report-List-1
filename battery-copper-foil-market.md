<p><p>Battery copper foil market types refer to the different thicknesses of copper foils used in various battery applications. Thin copper foil measures between 12 and 18 micrometers, and common copper foil falls within the range of 18 to 70 micrometers. Thick copper foil, on the other hand, exceeds 70 micrometers in thickness. The market segments these types to cater to different battery requirements in terms of conductivity, flexibility, and robustness.</p></p>
