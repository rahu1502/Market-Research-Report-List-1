<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerospace adhesive and sealants market consists of two main types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based adhesive and sealants are formulated with water as the primary solvent, making them environmentally-friendly and easy to clean up. They offer low volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions and are commonly used in applications where environmental regulations are stringent. On the other hand, solvent-based adhesive and sealants utilize organic solvents as the main carrier. They offer high adhesion and are preferred in demanding aerospace applications due to their quick curing and high bond strength capabilities.</p></p>
