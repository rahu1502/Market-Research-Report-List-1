<p><p>The LAN as a Service (LANaaS) market includes three types of services. Wi-Fi Only services focus solely on providing wireless connectivity to devices within a specific area. Management Only market offerings concentrate on overseeing and optimizing an existing LAN infrastructure while leaving the ownership and operation of the network hardware and software to the client.
