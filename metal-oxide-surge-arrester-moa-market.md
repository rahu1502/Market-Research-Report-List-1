<p><p>A Metal Oxide Surge Arrester (MOA) is a protective device used in electrical systems to limit voltage spikes and prevent damage to sensitive equipment. When a surge occurs, the MOA quickly diverts excess current to the ground, safeguarding the connected devices.</p><p>The Metal Oxide Surge Arrester (MOA) market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing in the coming years.
