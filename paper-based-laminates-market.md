<p><p>Paper Based Laminates are categorized into two major types: Low Pressure Laminates and High Pressure Laminates. Low Pressure Laminates are typically used in furniture, flooring, and interior decorations. These laminates are created by applying adhesive and a decorative paper layer to a substrate at a relatively low pressure. On the other hand, High Pressure Laminates are commonly utilized in countertops, cabinets, and wall panels. In this type, decorative paper layers are impregnated with melamine resin and compressed under high pressure to form a strong and durable product.</p></p>
