<p><p>The clothing steamer market can be classified into two main types: floor-standing and handheld. Floor-standing steamers are larger in size and often have a base unit with a water tank and a long hose attachment for steaming clothes hanging on a rack or curtains. They are suitable for larger garment volumes and are commonly used in professional settings like boutiques or dry cleaning shops. On the other hand, handheld steamers are smaller and more compact, designed for portability and convenience. They are ideal for personal use, allowing users to remove wrinkles from clothes quickly and efficiently, and are often preferred for travel or home use.</p></p>
