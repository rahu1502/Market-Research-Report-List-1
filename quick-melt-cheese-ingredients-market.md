<p><p>Quick-melt cheese ingredients market types include milk, cultures, enzymes, and additives. Milk is the primary ingredient in cheese production, providing the necessary proteins and fats. Cultures are added to ferment the milk, converting lactose into lactic acid and giving the cheese its flavor and texture. Enzymes, such as rennet, aid in curdling the milk and separating the curds from the whey. Additives like sodium citrate or sodium phosphate may be used to enhance the melting properties and shelf life of the cheese. These ingredients play crucial roles in creating quick-melt cheese products.</p></p>
