<p><p>The Prismatic Li-ion battery market consists of three main types: LiCoO2 Battery, LiFePO4 Battery, and NMC/NCA Battery. LiCoO2 Battery, made with lithium cobalt oxide cathode, offers high energy density but is prone to overheating. LiFePO4 Battery, comprising lithium iron phosphate, has a lower energy density but offers better thermal stability and longer lifespan. NMC/NCA Battery, incorporating lithium nickel manganese cobalt oxide or lithium nickel cobalt aluminum oxide, strikes a balance between energy density and safety with improved stability and longevity. These different battery types cater to diverse applications and the varying needs of industries.</p></p>
