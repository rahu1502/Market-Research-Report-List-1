<p><p>The sheet music market is divided into two main types: digital and in paper. Digital sheet music refers to musical notation that is accessed and displayed electronically, often through online platforms or software applications. It provides convenience and accessibility to musicians, as it can be easily stored, shared, and played on digital devices. On the other hand, in paper sheet music refers to the traditional method of printing and distributing physical copies of musical notation. While it may lack the digital benefits, it continues to serve those who prefer the tangible experience of reading and playing from printed sheet music.</p></p>
