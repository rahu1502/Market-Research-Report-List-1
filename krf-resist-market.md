<p><p>The KrF resist market consists of two types: Positive KrF resist and Negative KrF resist. Positive KrF resist is a type of resist that becomes more soluble when exposed to KrF radiation, and is commonly used in the manufacturing of microprocessors. On the other hand, Negative KrF resist is a type of resist that becomes less soluble when exposed to KrF radiation. It is mainly utilized in the production of printed circuit boards. Both types serve different purposes in the semiconductor industry and play crucial roles in the fabrication process.</p></p>
