<p><p>The fixed multi gas detector market includes various types of detectors such as catalytic combustion, electrochemical (echem), infrared (IR), and photoionization detectors (PID). Catalytic combustion detectors detect gases by measuring the heat released during their combustion. Electrochemical detectors use chemical reactions to detect and measure gases. Infrared detectors use the absorption of infrared radiation by gases to identify their presence. Photoionization detectors work by ionizing gas molecules using ultraviolet light and then measuring the resulting ion current. These detectors are widely used to monitor and detect multiple gases in industrial settings.</p></p>
