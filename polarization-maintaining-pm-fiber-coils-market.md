<p><p>Polarization Maintaining (PM) Fiber Coils are specialized optical fibers that maintain the polarization state of light as it travels through the fiber. These fibers are designed to minimize or eliminate polarization mode dispersion (PMD), which can occur in standard optical fibers and lead to signal degradation in certain applications.</p><p>PM Fiber Coils are vital components in various industries, including telecommunications, aerospace, defense, and instrumentation. They are commonly used in applications such as fiber optic gyroscopes, interferometers, and high-speed optical communications where the preservation of polarization is crucial for accurate and reliable signal transmission.</p><p>The future outlook for the Polarization Maintaining (PM) Fiber Coils market is expected to be promising.
