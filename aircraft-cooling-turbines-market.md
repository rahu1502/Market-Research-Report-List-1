<p><p>The Aircraft Cooling Turbines market includes various types of cooling turbines used in aircraft engines. Fan Cooling Turbines are designed specifically to cool the aircraft engine by extracting cool air from the fan section. Compressor Cooling Turbines, on the other hand, provide cooling by using bleed air from the engine compressor section. Lastly, Compressor Fan Cooling Turbines combine the functionalities of both fan and compressor turbines to enhance the cooling efficiency of the aircraft engine. These turbines play a crucial role in maintaining optimal temperatures within the engine for efficient operation.</p></p>
