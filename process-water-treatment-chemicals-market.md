<p><p>Process water treatment chemicals are used to treat water in various industrial processes to ensure its quality and prevent any harmful effects. These chemicals can be classified into different types based on their specific functions. Corrosion inhibitors are used to prevent the degradation of metal equipment. Scale inhibitors are used to prevent the formation of mineral scales. Coagulants are used to clump together suspended particles for easier removal. Flocculants are used to agglomerate particles and improve solid-liquid separation. Other types of chemicals may include pH adjusters, disinfectants, and oxidants, among others.</p></p>
