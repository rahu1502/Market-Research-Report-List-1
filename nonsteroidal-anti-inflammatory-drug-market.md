<p><strong>The Nonsteroidal Anti-inflammatory Drug Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Nonsteroidal Anti-inflammatory Drug (NSAID) market can be categorized into Type I and Type II. Type I includes drugs like ibuprofen and aspirin, which act by inhibiting the COX-1 enzyme and are available over-the-counter. Type II comprises newer drugs such as celecoxib, which selectively target COX-2 enzyme and require a prescription. Both types are widely used for pain relief and reducing inflammation but differ in their mechanism of action, availability, and level of regulation. Type II NSAIDs have gained popularity due to their perceived lower risk of gastrointestinal side effects compared to Type I NSAIDs.</p></p>
