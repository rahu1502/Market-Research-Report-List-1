<p><p>Waste collection trucks serve the purpose of collecting and transporting waste. There are several types of waste collection trucks available in the market. Front loaders are equipped with a hydraulic lift at the front to empty dumpsters into the truck. Rear loaders have a hydraulic compactor at the rear for compacting waste. Side loaders can collect waste from the curbside using a mechanical arm. Pneumatic collection trucks use suction to collect waste. Grapple trucks are used for large and bulky waste items, while liquid tanker trucks transport liquid waste materials.</p></p>
