<p><p>LDPE resins are widely used in the extrusion coating industry due to their excellent properties like flexibility, transparency, and moisture resistance. Two common methods used for producing LDPE resins are the tubular process and autoclave process. The tubular process involves the polymerization of ethylene in a tubular reactor using a catalyst, while the autoclave process involves high-pressure polymerization of ethylene in an autoclave reactor. Both processes result in LDPE resins with similar characteristics, making them suitable for various applications in the extrusion coating market.</p></p>
