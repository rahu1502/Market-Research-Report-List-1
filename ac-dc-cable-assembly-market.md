<p><p>The AC-DC cable assembly market is divided into four types: flexible cables, semi-flexible cables, semi-rigid cables, and rigid cables. Flexible cables are highly pliable and can be bent without any damage to the cable itself. Semi-flexible cables have moderate pliability, allowing for some bending but with less flexibility than flexible cables. Semi-rigid cables have minimal flexibility and are primarily used in applications that require stability and precision. Rigid cables have zero flexibility and are used in applications where a fixed connection is required.</p></p>
