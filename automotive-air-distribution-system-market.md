<p><p>The automotive air distribution system market includes two types: conventional air ducts and twinsheet thermoformed air ducts. Conventional air ducts are typically made of plastic or metal and are designed to direct the flow of air from the HVAC system to different areas of the vehicle cabin. On the other hand, twinsheet thermoformed air ducts are manufactured using a special plastic forming technique, providing a lightweight and more efficient solution for air distribution in vehicles. Both types of air ducts play a crucial role in ensuring proper ventilation and comfort in automobiles.</p></p>
