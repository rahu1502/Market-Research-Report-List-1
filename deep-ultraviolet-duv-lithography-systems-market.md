<p><p>The Deep Ultraviolet (DUV) Lithography Systems Market is segmented into three types: ArF Immersion, ArF Dry, and KrF. ArF Immersion lithography uses water as an immersion medium to achieve a higher resolution in the lithographic process. It is commonly used for advanced semiconductor manufacturing. ArF Dry lithography, on the other hand, does not require a liquid immersion medium and is suitable for different applications, including advanced packaging and microelectromechanical systems. Lastly, KrF lithography uses a krypton fluoride laser for printing smaller feature sizes and is commonly used in memory and logic chip manufacturing.</p></p>
