<p><p>The atomized copper based powder market is categorized into two main types: water atomized and air atomized. Water atomized copper powder is obtained by spraying water onto molten copper, causing it to rapidly solidify into fine particles. This type of powder is typically used in applications that require high purity and relatively low oxygen content. On the other hand, air atomized copper powder is created by using compressed air to disperse molten copper into a powder form. This type of powder is often employed in applications that prioritize good flowability and surface finish.</p></p>
