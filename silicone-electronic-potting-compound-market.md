<p><p>The silicone electronic potting compound market includes two main types: condensed type and addition type. Condensed type compounds are made through the process of condensation polymerization, resulting in a rigid and brittle material. On the other hand, addition type compounds are created by addition polymerization, leading to a flexible and elastic material. Both types are used for encapsulating and protecting electronic components from environmental factors such as moisture, dust, and vibrations, ensuring their long-term functionality and reliability.</p></p>
