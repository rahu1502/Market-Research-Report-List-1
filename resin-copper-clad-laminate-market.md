<p><p>The Resin Copper Clad Laminate (CCL) market comprises various types such as PTFE, PPO/PPE, Hydrocarbon Resin, LCP, BMI, and others. PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) is known for its high thermal conductivity and low dielectric constant. PPO/PPE (polyphenylene oxide/polyphenylene ether) offers remarkable electrical insulation properties and heat resistance. Hydrocarbon Resin displays excellent mechanical strength and resistance to chemicals. LCP (liquid crystal polymer) is characterized by low water absorption and high-temperature resistance. BMI (bismaleimide) offers exceptional mechanical and electrical properties. The "others" category includes additional types that provide specific attributes for diverse applications within the Resin CCL market.</p></p>
