<p><p>TPMS (Tire Pressure Monitoring System) battery refers to the small battery that powers the sensor within a TPMS unit. This sensor is responsible for monitoring the tire pressure and alerting the driver about any underinflated or overinflated tires. The TPMS battery plays a crucial role in ensuring accurate and reliable tire pressure readings. Over time, these batteries can lose their charge or deteriorate, leading to decreased performance of the TPMS unit.</p><p>The TPMS battery market is witnessing significant growth due to various factors. Firstly, the increasing adoption of TPMS in automobiles across the globe is driving the demand for TPMS batteries. Government regulations mandating the installation of TPMS in vehicles to enhance safety and fuel efficiency further contribute to market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the rising demand for electric vehicles (EVs) is also boosting the TPMS battery market. EVs are equipped with TPMS, which necessitates the need for reliable and long-lasting batteries to power the sensors.</p><p>In terms of market trends, the industry is witnessing advancements in battery technology to improve performance and longevity.
