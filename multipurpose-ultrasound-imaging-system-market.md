<p><p>The multipurpose ultrasound imaging system market can be classified into two types: black and white (B/W) and color systems. B/W ultrasound systems produce grayscale images that show the internal structures of organs and tissues. These systems are primarily used in areas such as cardiology, obstetrics, and emergency medicine. On the other hand, color ultrasound systems use Doppler technology to create color-coded images that depict blood flow and vascular structures. These systems are commonly used in radiology, vascular medicine, and other specialties requiring detailed visualization of blood vessels.</p></p>
