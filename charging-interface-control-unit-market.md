<p><p>The Charging Interface Control Unit (CICU) market can be categorized into two types: DC Control Unit and AC Control Unit. A DC Control Unit is designed to facilitate the charging of electric vehicles (EVs) by converting alternating current (AC) into direct current (DC). It acts as a translator between the EV and the charging station. On the other hand, an AC Control Unit enables the charging of EVs directly from an AC power source. It transforms the AC power into an appropriate voltage level for the EV's battery. Both types of CICUs play a crucial role in ensuring efficient and safe charging of EVs.</p></p>
