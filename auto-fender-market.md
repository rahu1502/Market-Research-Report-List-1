<p><p>The auto fender market consists of two main applications: OEM and aftermarket. These fenders are made according to specific vehicle models and are of higher quality. Aftermarket fenders, on the other hand, are produced by third-party manufacturers and are intended to replace damaged or worn-out fenders in existing vehicles. These fenders offer a wider range of options and are generally more affordable than OEM fenders.</p></p>
