<p><p>High voltage cables in the EV market are categorized into two types: shielded cables and unshielded cables. Shielded cables have an extra protective layer made of conductive material to minimize electromagnetic interference and ensure safety. They are commonly used in high-power applications to prevent signal degradation. Unshielded cables, on the other hand, lack this additional layer and are mainly used in low-power applications where electromagnetic interference is not a major concern. Both types cater to different requirements and preferences in the EV market.</p></p>
