<p><p>The CNC automation system market encompasses three major types: turning, milling, and grinding. Turning involves the rotation of a workpiece on a lathe while a cutting tool shapes it to the desired form. Milling utilizes rotary cutters to remove material from the workpiece, achieving the required shape or surface. Grinding, on the other hand, employs abrasive wheels to attain precise finishes or dimensions. All three types serve different purposes in the CNC automation system market, catering to various industries and applications.</p></p>
