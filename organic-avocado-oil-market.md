<p><p>The organic avocado oil market consists of three main types: refined, virgin, and extra virgin. Refined avocado oil undergoes a process that eliminates impurities and enhances its taste, making it suitable for high-temperature cooking. Virgin avocado oil is extracted through mechanical means, without the use of chemicals, and has a milder flavor and aroma. Extra virgin avocado oil is the highest quality and extracted using cold-pressing methods, retaining the maximum nutrients and flavor. It is best used in dishes that require a delicate, fruity taste, such as salads or as a finishing oil.</p></p>
