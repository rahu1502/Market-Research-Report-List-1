<p><p>The automotive privacy window film market consists of three types: stained film, primary color film, and vacuum coating film. Stained film is created by adding dyes or pigments to the film material, giving it a tinted appearance. Primary color film is made using colored polyester material, providing a variety of vibrant colors. Vacuum coating film is manufactured by applying metal coatings to the film's surface, resulting in a reflective appearance and enhanced solar performance. These different types of films offer various options for consumers looking to enhance privacy and style in their vehicles.</p></p>
