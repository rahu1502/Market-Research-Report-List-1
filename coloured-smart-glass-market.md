<p><p>Coloured smart glass refers to a type of glass that can change its tint or color based on external conditions. Electrochromic smart glass utilizes an electric current to control the color and transparency of the glass. SPD smart glass contains suspended particles that align or disperse in response to an electric current, altering the tint. PDLC smart glass employs a polymer film that can switch between opaque and transparent states when an electric current is applied. These various types of smart glass offer dynamic and customizable tinting options for architectural and automotive applications.</p></p>
