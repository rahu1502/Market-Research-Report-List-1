<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Pressure-side pool cleaners are classified into two market types: booster pump and filter pump. A booster pump system requires an additional pump to provide the required water pressure for the cleaner to operate effectively. This type is more powerful and efficient, capable of removing larger debris. On the other hand, a filter pump system utilizes the existing pool filtration system to generate pressure for the cleaner, making it a more cost-effective option.</p></p>
