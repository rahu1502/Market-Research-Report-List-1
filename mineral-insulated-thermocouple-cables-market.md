<p><p>The mineral insulated thermocouple cables market offers three main types: two conductors (simplex), four conductors (duplex), and others. Two conductor cables consist of a single pair of thermocouple wires, making them ideal for basic temperature measurement applications. Four conductor cables include two pairs of thermocouple wires, offering better accuracy and built-in redundancy for critical temperature sensing requirements. The "others" category comprises alternative designs and configurations that may include additional conductors or unique features to suit specific industrial or commercial applications beyond the simplex and duplex options.</p></p>
