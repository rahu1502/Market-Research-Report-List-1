<p><p>Crosslinked Polyvinylpolypyrrolidone (PVPP) is a white, yellowish or slightly grey powder, which is a highly crosslinked derivative of polyvinylpyrrolidone (PVP). PVPP is soluble in water and other polar solvents, and it is widely used as a clarifying agent, stabilizer, and adsorbent in various industries including pharmaceuticals, food and beverages, cosmetics, and industrial applications.</p><p>In the pharmaceutical industry, PVPP is primarily used as a binder in tablet formulations to improve drug dissolution and bioavailability. It is also utilized for the removal of impurities and unwanted substances from the drug formulations. In the food and beverage industry, PVPP is employed as a clarification agent in beer production to remove unwanted proteins and polyphenols, resulting in improved product stability and shelf life.</p><p>The robust growth in the pharmaceutical and food and beverage industries is expected to drive the demand for PVPP in the coming years.
