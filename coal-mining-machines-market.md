<p><p>The coal mining machines market can be classified into four main types. Frame type machines are widely used and consist of a frame structure with a cutting head and conveyor system. Drum type machines have rotating drums with cutting teeth that excavate coal. Standing roller machines use a roller to cut the coal and then convey it to an external device. Drilling type machines employ drilling techniques to extract coal. Each type offers different advantages and is used based on the specific requirements of the mining operation.</p></p>
