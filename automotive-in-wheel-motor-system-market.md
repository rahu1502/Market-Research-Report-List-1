<p><p>The Automotive In-Wheel Motor System market includes two primary types of motors: DC Motors and AC Motors. DC Motors are powered by direct current and commonly exhibit efficient power transmission, making them suitable for electric vehicles. However, they require additional components for conversion of AC power from the battery. AC Motors, on the other hand, use alternating current and offer improved power efficiency. They eliminate the need for additional conversion devices, making them advantageous for in-wheel motor systems, ultimately enhancing vehicle performance and reducing energy consumption.</p></p>
