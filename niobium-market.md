<p><p>Niobium market types can be categorized into three main segments: ferroniobium, niobium oxide, and niobium metal. Ferroniobium is an alloy composed of iron and niobium used mainly in steel production to improve its strength and corrosion resistance. Niobium oxide, also known as niobium pentoxide, is a compound often used in the production of capacitors, optical glasses, and ceramics. Lastly, niobium metal is a highly malleable and ductile element used in various industries, including aerospace, electronics, and superconductors, due to its excellent heat resistance and conductivity.</p></p>
