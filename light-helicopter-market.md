<p><p>The light helicopter market comprises two main types: the piston-engined helicopter and the turboshaft-engined helicopter. The piston-engined helicopter is powered by a piston engine, offering simplicity, lower cost, and easier maintenance. It is commonly used for personal, recreational or training purposes. On the other hand, the turboshaft-engined helicopter utilizes a turboshaft engine, offering higher power, enhanced performance, and the ability to carry heavier loads. It is typically used for commercial, military, or emergency services, where greater capacity and versatility are required.</p></p>
