<p><p>Razor wire has two main market types: Concertina Razor Wire and Flat Razor Wire. </p><p>Concertina Razor Wire features sharp blades tightly spiraled around a central core, creating a coiled structure. It is highly effective in preventing intrusion and widely used in military and high-security areas. </p><p>Flat Razor Wire, on the other hand, consists of sharp blades arranged in a flat manner. This type of razor wire is often used in commercial and residential settings, providing a visible deterrent against trespassing and enhancing perimeter security.</p></p>
