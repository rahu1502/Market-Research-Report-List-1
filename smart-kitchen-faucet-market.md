<p><p>The Smart Kitchen Faucet market consists of two main types: Touch and Contactless. Touch faucets allow users to easily turn the water on or off by simply touching the faucet with their hand or forearm. These faucets are designed to provide convenience and efficient water flow control. On the other hand, contactless faucets use advanced sensor technology to detect the presence of hands or objects, allowing users to operate the faucet without any physical contact. This type of faucet promotes hygiene by reducing the risk of cross-contamination and is gaining popularity in modern kitchens.</p></p>
