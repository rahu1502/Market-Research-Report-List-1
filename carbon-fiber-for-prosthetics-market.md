<p><p>The Carbon Fiber for Prosthetics Market consists of three main types: Conventional, Electric Powered, and Hybrid Orthopedic Prosthetics. Conventional prosthetics refer to the standard devices that mimic the functioning of a missing limb through passive mechanisms. Electric powered prosthetics use motors and sensors to enhance the natural movement of the limb. Hybrid orthopedic prosthetics combine elements of both conventional and electric powered prosthetics, providing a balance between functionality and cost-effectiveness. These different types cater to the diverse needs of amputees, offering varying degrees of mobility and comfort.</p></p>
