<p><p>Active Metal Brazed (AMB) substrates are a type of substrate material used in various industries, especially in the electronics and automotive sectors. These substrates are composed of multiple layers of metals and ceramics that are joined together using active metal brazing techniques. The active metal brazing process involves melting a filler material containing an active metal component, such as titanium, into a joint to bond the different layers of the substrate.</p><p>The AMB substrates offer several advantages over traditional substrates, including excellent thermal conductivity, high mechanical strength, and good electrical insulation properties. These attributes make them suitable for applications requiring high heat dissipation, such as power electronics, LED lighting, and automotive electronics.</p><p>The future outlook for the AMB substrates market is promising, driven by the increasing demand for electronic devices and the need for efficient thermal management solutions. The growing adoption of electric vehicles and the development of advanced electronic systems, including 5G infrastructure and autonomous vehicles, are expected to further boost the demand for AMB substrates.</p><p>Moreover, the increasing focus on green energy and the rapid growth of renewable energy sources, such as wind and solar power, also create opportunities for the AMB substrates market.
