<p><p>Cementing additives market includes various types of additives that are used in cementing operations in the oil and gas industry. Cement retarders are added to slow down the setting time of cement, allowing more time for proper placement. Cement dispersants are used to improve the flow ability of the cement slurry. Fluid loss control agents help prevent the loss of water from the slurry during placement. Cement spacer and flushes are used to clean the wellbore and improve cement bond. Other additives include accelerators, extenders, and defoamers, among others.</p></p>
