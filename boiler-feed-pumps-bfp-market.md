<p><p>Boiler Feed Pumps (BFP) are devices used to supply water to boilers in power plants and industrial facilities. There are two main types: Vertical Boiler Feed Pumps and Horizontal Boiler Feed Pumps. Vertical BFPs are designed with a vertically arranged motor and pump unit, making them suitable for compact spaces. Horizontal BFPs, on the other hand, have a horizontally arranged motor and pump unit, providing higher operational flexibility. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring continuous water supply to boilers, preventing overheating and damage.</p></p>
