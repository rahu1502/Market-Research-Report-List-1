<p><p>Thermostatic Radiator Valves (TRVs) are devices used in heating systems to control the temperature of individual radiators in a space. There are two main types of TRVs available in the market. Self-operate TRVs are mechanical valves that regulate the flow of hot water based on the ambient temperature. They do not require external power or controls. On the other hand, Electronic TRVs are more advanced and electronically controlled.
