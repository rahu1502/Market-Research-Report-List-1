<p><p>Video streaming media software market can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based solutions enable users to stream and access videos through the internet on various devices. It offers scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness as the software and infrastructure are hosted and managed by a third-party provider. On the other hand, on-premises solutions require users to install and maintain the software on their local servers. This provides users with more control over their video streaming operations but requires higher upfront costs and ongoing maintenance efforts.</p></p>
