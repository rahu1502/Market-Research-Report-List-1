<p><p>A thermal transfer receipt printer is a device that uses a thermal print head to transfer ink onto thermal paper. The printer works by heating up the print head, which then melts the ink on the ribbon and transfers it onto the paper.</p><p>The thermal transfer receipt printer market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
