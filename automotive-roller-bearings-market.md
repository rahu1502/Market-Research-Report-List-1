<p><p>Automotive roller bearings are classified into various types based on their design and application. Cylindrical roller bearings possess high radial load capacity and are suitable for applications with high-speed rotation. Tapered roller bearings can support both radial and axial loads, making them ideal for wheel assemblies. Needle roller bearings are compact and offer high load capacity for limited space applications. The "Others" category includes specialty roller bearings, such as spherical roller bearings, which can accommodate misalignment. These options provide automotive manufacturers with a wide range of choices to meet their specific needs.</p></p>
