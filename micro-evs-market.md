<p><p>Micro EVs refer to small electric vehicles designed for short-distance commutes. The market can be categorized into Lead-acid Battery EVs and Lithium-ion Battery EVs. Lead-acid Battery EVs use traditional lead-acid batteries as their power source. These vehicles are usually less expensive but have a shorter range and longer charging time. On the other hand, Lithium-ion Battery EVs utilize advanced lithium-ion batteries, offering longer range and shorter charging time. Although they are more expensive, they provide improved performance and energy efficiency compared to lead-acid Battery EVs.</p></p>
