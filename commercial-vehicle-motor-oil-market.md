<p><p>The commercial vehicle motor oil market includes two major types: mineral oil and synthetic oil. Mineral oil is derived from crude oil and undergoes a refining process to remove impurities. It is cost-effective and widely used in standard commercial vehicles. On the other hand, synthetic oil is artificially produced using chemical compounds. It offers enhanced lubrication performance, better protection against high temperatures, and prolonged engine life, making it suitable for heavy-duty vehicles or those subjected to extreme conditions. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific needs and requirements of the vehicles.</p></p>
