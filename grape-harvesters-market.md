<p><p>Grape harvesters are agricultural machines used to harvest grapes quickly and efficiently. Two popular types in the market are the Pivotal Striker and Trunk Shaker. The Pivotal Striker harvester has a rotating head that gently strikes the vines, shaking off the grapes, which then fall onto a conveyor belt. In contrast, the Trunk Shaker uses clamps to shake the entire vine, which dislodges the grapes that are collected through a catching frame. Both harvesters offer different approaches to grape harvesting, catering to farmers' specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
