<p><p>The bicycle brake components market consists of various types of brakes. Spoon brake is a basic design, while duck brake has two arms resembling a duck's beak. Rim brake uses pads that press against the wheel's rim, while disc brake uses pads that squeeze a rotor attached to the wheel hub. Drum brake has internal shoes pressing against a drum within the wheel, and coaster brake lets riders pedal backward to engage the brake. Drag brake is used in mountain bikes for controlled downhill descents, and band brake is a simple mechanism using a friction band to stop the bicycle.</p></p>
