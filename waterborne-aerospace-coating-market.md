<p><p>Waterborne aerospace coating refers to a type of paint or coating that is specifically designed and formulated for application on aircraft and other aerospace vehicles. Waterborne aerospace coatings provide excellent corrosion and weather resistance, as well as superior adhesion and durability.</p><p>The future outlook for the waterborne aerospace coating market is highly positive. The increasing demand for eco-friendly and sustainable coatings, along with stringent government regulations regarding VOC emissions, is driving the market growth.
