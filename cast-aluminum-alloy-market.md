<p><p>Cast aluminum alloy is a type of aluminum alloy that is produced through the casting process, which involves pouring molten metal into a mold to obtain the desired shape. In the aerospace industry, these alloys are used in manufacturing aircraft parts, including wings, fuselages, and landing gear.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing trend of electrification in the automotive industry is expected to fuel the demand for cast aluminum alloys.
